Lasagna

A recipe on how to make the best lasagna, apparently.


Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

      Make the meat sauce
      Cook the noodles
      Make the ricotta mixture.
      Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
      Cover with foil and bake.
      Let the lasagna rest before serving
